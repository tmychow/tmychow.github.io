Hey there! I'm Trevor - I'm a first year economist and Jardine Scholar at Trinity College, Cambridge. This website is a collection of my ramblings on economics and various other things, with a focus on macroeconomics. I post around once a fortnight and you can keep up with my posts by subscribing to my newsletter.

My 5 most recent posts are below, with the full blog archive available here. Some of my posts you may want to check out include: my interview with Nobel Laureate Oliver Hart, my three-part primer on economic growth, my summary of social democratic economic policy and my review of the open borders literature.

I'm always interested in opportunities to get involved with economic research, policy thinktanks, and policymaking - my resume is readily available here. Beyond that, I love hearing what people think about my blog and to connect with/learn from those with overlapping interests - as such, don't hesitate to get in touch by twitter or by email. I am busy at times, but I'll always make sure to respond in some way!
