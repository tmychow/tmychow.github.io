Hey there! I'm Trevor - I'm a first year economist and Jardine Scholar at Trinity College, Cambridge. This website is a collection of my ramblings on economics and various other things, with a focus on macroeconomics. I post around once a fortnight and you can keep up with my posts by subscribing to my newsletter.

My 5 most recent posts are at the bottom of this page, with the full blog archive available here. Some of my posts you may want to check out include:

  • My interview with Nobel Laureate Oliver Hart
  • My three-part introductory primer to economic growth theory
  • My take on why short-run fluctuations matter more than Lucas (2003) would suggest
  • My summary of social democratic economic policy
  • My review of the economic literature on open borders

I'm always interested in opportunities to get involved with economic research, policy thinktanks, and policymaking - my resume is readily available here, so feel free to get in touch. Beyond that, I love hearing what people think about my blog and connecting with those wjo have overlapping interests - as such, don't hesitate to message me on twitter or to contact me by email!
