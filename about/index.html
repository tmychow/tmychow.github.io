Who am I?

My Personal Background

Hey there! I'm Trevor Chow and I am an 18-year old from Hong Kong who studies in the UK. This website is a collection of my ramblings on economics and public policy. I post around once a fortnight - feel free to message me on twitter if you have any thoughts. I also co-run an instagram and a youtube account, both of which bring attention to important social issues across the globe.

My content is broadly arranged into four types - takes, effortposts, interviews and notes. Takes are short back-of-the-envelope thoughts I have that aren't terribly rigorous. Effortposts are pieces where I put a decent amount of time into research. Interviews are transcripts of my conversations with academics and policy people. Notes is a collection of things I've jotted down in the course of my reading.

Where do I stand?

My view on publi policy can be summarised as "neoliberal" and epistemically modest. It is a framework with a great deal of deference towards individual liberty, coupled with a healthy dose of evidence-based pragmatism.
